<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Published ProductEvent -> reconnect consumer | Concurrent publication crosses a replay/live cut and must preserve sequence evidence. |
| Cursor input -> retained deque | Caller-controlled sequence can refer to missing or future history. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-08-REPLAY-GAP | Tampering / Repudiation | recovery_boundary_after | high | mitigate | Single publication lock, exact sequence partition, barrier-controlled race test, structured requested/oldest evidence. |
| T-08-03-LAG-CONFLATION | Repudiation | ProductEventReceiver lag mapping | high | mitigate | Separate internal recovery variants and tests for retained-history gap versus live receiver lag. |
| T-08-03-DEADLOCK | Denial of Service | publication_state mutex | high | mitigate | No await/serialization under lock; critical section only subscribes, captures, validates, and clones bounded retained data. |
| T-08-03-CURSOR-FORGE | Tampering | requested sequence | medium | mitigate | Validate against current/oldest bounds and return typed recovery rather than silently skipping history. |
</threat_model>

## Artifacts this phase produces

- New private symbols in `event_service.rs`: `ProductEventRecoveryBoundary`, `ProductEventRecovery`, and `EventService::recovery_boundary_after`.
- New boundary fields: `requested_after`, `replayed_through`, `oldest_available`, `replay`, and `receiver` as applicable to the private variants.
- New cfg(test)-only recovery synchronization hook/barrier fixture and deterministic `recovery_*` tests.
- No new public type, file, dependency, client acknowledgement field, or adapter behavior is introduced in this plan.
